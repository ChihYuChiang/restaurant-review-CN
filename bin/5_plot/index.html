Restaurant Preference Map

This is a side project of Chih-Yu Chiang.
It aims to visualize individuals' preference toward popular restaurants in Taipei.

Instruction
  • A dot is a restaurant.
  • The proximity in the map shows the restaurants' similarity regarding their dining experience.
  • The deeper the color, the higher the estimated individual preference.
  • in the tips are the individual's actual preference.
  • Click the restaurant to zoom in and get the dining recommendation from its 5 most similar restaurant buddies.
Data
  • The dining-experience space is created on 5-million restaurant review articles scraped from dianping.com and pixnet.net.
  • These articles are used to update a GloVe embedding based on a 100M-token Weibo corpus.
  • The embedding is projected onto a 2D space via the t-sne algorithm. Upon the projection, 150 popular restaurants in Taipei were plotted.
  • Individuals report their preference toward 100 of these popular restaurants. Based on that, an overall preference density is estimated.
  • The visualization is created via D3.js.
